Gladius puts you into the role of a Gladiator fighting for the favor
of the Emperor! Create a Gladiator, chose your weapon and armor and
do battle with the champions of Rome. Win, and you will be rewarded
greatly, fail, and you could end up dead, or worse!

Gladius is your adventure into the Colosseum of Rome. You will face
10 of the fircest warriors Rome has ever sent into the arena to earn
praise and glory from the citizens and Emperor of Rome.  

Developed by David South for CSC 17A section 47975 at Riverside City
College Fall 2013. Parts of this program have been modified from 
the class textbook written by Tony Gaddis and from class lectures.
